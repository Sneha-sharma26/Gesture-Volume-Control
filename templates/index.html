How to use — Quick Steps

  1. Allow camera access when the browser asks.
  2. Press Start Camera to begin streaming.
  3. Use gestures (your model) to change volume — volume value appears left.
  4. Press Stop Camera to completely stop the stream and free the camera.

Helpful Tips & Best Practices

  • Good lighting: face the light source for clearer hand detection.
  • Neutral background reduces false detections.
  • Keep camera at about eye/shoulder height for steady framing.
  • Test in a quiet environment if you add audio feedback.
